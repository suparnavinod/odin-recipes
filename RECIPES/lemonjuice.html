Lemon Juice

Description

Lemon juice is a refreshing drink made from freshly squeezed lemons, sugar, water and a pinch of salt. It is easy to prepare and perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • water
  • sugar
  • salt
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Cut the lemons in half and squeeze out the juice into a bowl.
  2. Add salt and sugarto the lemon juice.
  3. Pour water and mix well untill the sugar dissolves.
  4. Add ice cubes if desired and serve chilled.
