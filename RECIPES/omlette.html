OMLETTE

Description

It is a soft and fluffy omlette with added veggies

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • onion
  • carrot
  • capsicum
  • salt
  • oil
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper, then whisk well.
  3. Add chopped vegetables( onion,capsicum, carrot etc )
  4. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Let it cook untill the edges start to set.
  7. Gently flip or fold the omlette
  8. Cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot.
