Banana Fritters

Description

Banana fritters, also known as pazhampori, are a popular Kerala tea time snack made with ripe bananas dipped in a lightly sweet batter and deep fried until golden and crispy.

Ingredients

  • bananas (riped)
  • all purpose flour
  • sugar
  • salt
  • oil
  • Turmeric powder
  • water

Steps

  1. Peel the ripe bananas and slice them lengthwise into thin pieces.
  2. In a bowl mix flour salt,sugar and a pinch of turmeric powder.
  3. Add water gradually and whisk to form a smooth, thick batter.
  4. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame.
  5. Dip each banana slice into the batter,coating it evenly.
  6. Deep fry the slices untill golden brown.
  7. Take out and serve hot with tea.
